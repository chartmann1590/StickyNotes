This policy explains how the Sticky Notes Android app handles data.

Local Note Data

Your notes are stored locally on your own device. The app does not require account sign-in for note storage.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app may display ads using Google AdMob (native, banner, and interstitial ads). Ad providers may process identifiers and related data to serve and measure ads.

Google privacy information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Consent

Where required, the app uses Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage consent options.

Crash and Performance Monitoring

The app may use Firebase services (such as Crashlytics, Analytics, and Performance Monitoring) to improve stability and usability.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intentionally directed to children under the applicable legal age threshold in your region.
