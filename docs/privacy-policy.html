Privacy policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026. This policy describes the Sticky Notes Android app (“the app”).

What you write in the app

Your note text is stored locally on your device in private app storage. It is not sent to our servers because we do not operate account-based cloud storage for this app.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app may show ads using Google AdMob, including native, banner, and interstitial formats. Google may use device identifiers and related data to deliver and measure ads. See Google’s privacy information for how Google uses data:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Where required, the app uses the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to help present consent choices for ads.

Analytics and stability (Firebase)

The app may use Google Firebase services such as Analytics, Crashlytics, and Performance Monitoring to understand usage trends, diagnose crashes, and improve performance. These services process app-related events according to Google’s policies.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region). If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, contact us so we can help you remove it from vendor systems where possible.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
