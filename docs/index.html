Sticky Notes

A simple Android sticky note with a home screen widget, modern Material design, and optional ads.

Support on Buy Me a Coffee Privacy policy

Features

  • Fast note editor with autosave to device storage
  • Home screen widget that mirrors your note
  • Share and clear from the toolbar menu
  • Native and banner ads (release builds use your AdMob units)
